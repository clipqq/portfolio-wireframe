About me.

Hello there.

My name is Cliff and I'm a web developer in the SF bay area. I graduated from UC Irvine and Thinkful's Engineering bootcamp.

I take pride in being able to learn anything fast.

Outside of coding I enjoy skiing, playing guitar, rafting whitewater, and board games like chess and go.

Mastering the fix & why I code.

When I was a grade school kid, I used to run up against installation errors all the time with PC games.

Probably like a lot of kids that would eventually grow up to be "power users", I spent countless hours Googling stuff about DirectX, driver updates, or whatever weird error message I get out of Windows XP.

Once in a while I manage to find the answer and the program finally boots up.

And there's no sound.

So, I spend even more time on the thing to get it working. At the end, it's amazing to see the pieces come together.

That's the feeling that keeps me going. Mastering the fix.
